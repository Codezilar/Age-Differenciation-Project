What is Age Difference?

The age difference is the difference between two ages. It is the measure where it defines the difference between two ages. It can be easily calculated by taking the difference beween two Dates, Months, and Years. If the both entities are the same, then there is no difference in age. For example, if Samson was born on 15th January 1998, and Ibrahim was born on 3rd, July 1995, then the age difference between Samson and Ibrahim is 2years, 6months and 13days respectively.
